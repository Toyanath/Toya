A Brief Introduction to HAOT

The acronym of HAOT is Himalayan Aromatic Arthodox Tea.It was established in 2015. Our main motto is to provide the original taste of
organic green tea to connoisseur of green and orthodox tea.Its main factory is located in Suryodaya municipiality Fikkal,Ilam,Nepal. We
are collecting the finished organic tea product form our factory and distributing from our four outlets in Japan. Tanakamonmachi Kyoto. There are various green tea made products that we are supplying in the markets. We are categorizing our products under
three main group, those are products specially targeted for man's use,Womens use and chield use. .
